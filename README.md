 🏦 Bank Customer Churn Risk Prediction

 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on predicting customer churn in the banking sector using Machine Learning and Risk Scoring techniques. The objective is to identify customers who are likely to leave the bank and assign a Risk Score and Risk Probability to support customer retention strategies.

An interactive Tableau dashboard has also been developed to visualize customer behavior, churn patterns, and risk distribution.

---

 🎯 Project Objectives

- Predict customer churn using Machine Learning.
- Generate Risk Probability for every customer.
- Assign Risk Scores to identify high-risk customers.
- Analyze customer behavior through interactive dashboards.
- Help banks improve customer retention and decision-making.

---

 📊 Dataset Information

The dataset contains customer information such as:

- Credit Score
- Geography
- Gender
- Age
- Tenure
- Balance
- Number of Products
- Credit Card Status
- Active Member Status
- Estimated Salary
- Customer Churn (Exited)

 🤖 Machine Learning Models

The following models were developed and evaluated:

- Logistic Regression
- Decision Tree Classifier
- Random Forest Classifier
- Gradient Boosting Classifier

Models were evaluated using:

- Accuracy
- Precision
- Recall
- F1-Score
- ROC-AUC Score



 Risk Scoring

The final model generates:

- Risk Probability
- Risk Score

These metrics help classify customers according to their likelihood of churn, enabling banks to prioritize retention efforts.
